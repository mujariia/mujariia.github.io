Call for Papers

DISCOVER solicits paper submissions across six tracks as listed below. Kindly note that the submissions are not limited to the topics mentioned in each track.

Track 1: Robotics and Industrial Automation  
  • Control and Automation
  • CAD and 3D Modelling
  • 3D Printing
  • Robotics
  • Robot planning, adaptation and learning
  • Internet of Robotic Things (IoRT)
  • Machine Vision and Perception
  • Industrial Robotics
  • Humanoid Robot
  • Human Centric Robotics
  • Soft Robotics 
  • AGV
  • Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)
  • Parallel and Serial Manipulator
  • Reconfigurable manipulators
  • Healthcare automation
Track 2: Industry 4.0 and 5.0  
  • Augmented Reality
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Digital Transformation
  • Human Machine Collaboration
  • Smart Manufacturing
  • Digital Twin
  • E-mobility and Additive Manufacturing
  • Collaborative Robots
 
  • Predictive Maintenance
  • Sustainable and Green Manufacturing
  • Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)
  • Data Analytics in Manufacturing
  • Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)
  • Smart Structures
  • Smart building and construction
  • Automation in agriculture and horticulture
Track 3: Bioengineering and Medical Technologies  
  • Biomechanics and Bio-robotics
  • Augmented and Virtual Reality
  • Biomedical Sensors and Wearable Systems
  • Robotics
  • Therapeutic and Diagnostic Systems and Technologies
  • Biomedical and Health Informatics
  • Translational Engineering for Healthcare Innovation and Commercialization
  • Biomedical Signal and Image Processing
  • Pattern Recognition
  • Nanomaterials
Track 4: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning  
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Quantum Computing
  • Computer Vision
  • AI in Agriculture, Healthcare and smart cities
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Generative AI
  • Robotic Process Automation, Intelligent Bots
  • Cloud-based automation
  • Big data, data mining and machine learning
  • Privacy and security in automation
  • Deep Learning
Track 5: Communication and Networking  
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Networks
  • 5G and Beyond
  • Cybersecurity in Networking
  • Blockchain in Communication
  • Edge Computing
  • Optical Communications and Networking
  • Wireless and Mobile Networks
  • Body Area Network
  • Cognitive Communications
  • Wireless Sensor Networks
